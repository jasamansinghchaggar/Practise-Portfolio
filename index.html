About Jasaman

Introduction

Hi, I'm Jasaman Singh, a Computer Science Engineering student at Arya College of Engineering. I am a passionate learner and a tech enthusiast. I love to explore new technologies and learn new things. I am a quick learner and a team player. I am always ready to take up new challenges and work on them. I am a hardworking person and I always try to give my best in everything I do.

Coding Journey

My coding journey started when I was in 11th grade. It all began with a curiosity to understand how computers work and the desire to create something of my own. I started learning the basics of programming languages like Python and studied MySQl and made some projects based on these two.

As I delved deeper into coding, I realized the power of problem-solving and the endless possibilities that programming offers. Then a new phase of my life started when I opted Computer Science Engineering degree at Arya College of Engineering, where I have started learning C programming. It has been an exciting journey so far, and I am eager to continue expanding my programming skills.

After gaining a solid foundation in programming, I decided to specialize in frontend development. I started by learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which are the building blocks of the web. I practiced creating static web pages and gradually moved on to more complex projects.
