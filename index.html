About Jasaman

Introduction

Jasaman Singh is a Computer Science Engineering student at Arya College of Engineering. He is a passionate learner and a tech enthusiast who loves to explore new technologies and learn new things. He is a quick learner and a team player, always ready to take up new challenges and work on them. He is a hardworking person who always tries to give his best in everything he does.

Coding Journey

His coding journey started when he was in 11th grade. It all began with a curiosity to understand how computers work and the desire to create something of his own. He started learning the basics of programming languages like Python and studied MySQL and made some projects based on these two.

As Jasaman delved deeper into coding, he realized the power of problem-solving and the endless possibilities that programming offers. Then a new phase of his life started when he opted for a Computer Science Engineering degree at Arya College of Engineering, where he started learning C programming. It has been an exciting journey so far, and he is eager to continue expanding his programming skills.

After gaining a solid foundation in programming, he decided to specialize in frontend development. He started by learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which are the building blocks of the web. He practiced creating static web pages and gradually moved on to more complex projects.
